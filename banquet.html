Banquet, Lawn & Convention

Celebrate in grandeur at our hotel's exquisite banquet hall and lush lawn. With impeccable service, stunning decor, and state-of-the-art facilities, our versatile spaces provide the perfect backdrop for unforgettable events, from elegant weddings to corporate gatherings. Experience extraordinary moments in the lap of luxury at our exceptional banquet and lawn venues.

Step into the majestic Claudius, our grand banquet hall spanning an impressive 50,000 sq. feet at our hotel. With its opulent ambiance and expansive space, sets the stage for unforgettable gatherings and extravagant events that leave a lasting impression.

Our dedicated event planning team will work closely with you to bring your vision to life, ensuring every detail is meticulously crafted to perfection. From delectable culinary creations prepared by our world-class chefs to seamless personalized service, your guests will be treated to an unforgettable experience. Trust us to create cherished memories in our prestigious banquet hall, where sophistication meets grandeur, and every moment is an extraordinary celebration.

Each of our guest rooms feature a private bath, wi-fi, television and include full breakfast.

Cancellation Policy
Cancellation Charges for individual bookings
  • If cancelled 96 hours prior to the arrival date full refund will be processed. There will be 5% cancellation charges in case payment is done via Credit/Debit card.
  • If cancelled within 48-72 hours prior to check-in date, Then 50% amount would be deducted if 100% advance received.
  • If cancelled within 24 hours prior to check-in date, Then 100% amount would be deducted if 100% advance received.
  • If no show there would not be any refund.
Confirmation/cancellation policy for weddings and large celebrations
  • 50% of the total estimated billing amount has to be made at the time of the confirmation of the event / function / large celebrations / wedding booking along with signed copy of function agreement mentioning terms & conditions.
  • 25% of the total estimated billing amount has to be made 75 - 90 days prior to event / function date.
  • Last 25% of the total estimated billing amount has to be made 45 - 60 days prior to event / function date.
  • If power billing of venues for example :- Claudius / Colossal / Cove / Cora Lawns, it has to be paid 30 days prior to function date based on function schedule or estimated requirement as per venue. And a copy of function schedule has to be intimated to hotel in a well advance.
  • If not complied with the above, the booking can be cancelled, and it would be non – refundable / non – transferable / non – adjustable to any other function in any circumstances whatsoever may be the reason.
  • If there is any such unknown incidents, then we would be able to give you another reschedule date subject to availability of venue, that would be valid for within 6 months only, as per management decision (subject to approval).
  • Full clearance of bill at the time of check-out.
  • In case of any cancellation:
    • The advance received for all the events are non - refundable and non - transferable whatsoever may be the circumstances / reason.
    • Cancellation of any Function must be notified to the hotel in WRITING Only.

Rules and Regulations
  • At the time of Check-in, every individual guest staying in the hotel is required to present a valid government identity proof which mentions the residential address. In case of foreign national, valid passport along with visa is required. Also the guests are requested to carry the reservation l etter at the time of arrival.
  • Total room rent is payable in advance in order to confirm the bookings. For weddings & other large functions, 100% of the approximate total billing is to be paid 45 - 60 days in advance in order to confirm the booking.
  • Outside alcohol and eatables are not allowed in the hotel.
  • Hookahs and personal music/speaker systems are not allowed in the hotel.
  • Lockers for your valuables are available in each guest room. The hotel shall not be responsible for any loss of or damage to your personal belongings.
  • In case any damage is done to the hotel property by guests during their stay, it will be the sole accountability of the guest that made the booking and Hukam's Lalit Mahal will levy damage charges as deemed fit.
  • The properties can be chartered for an exclusive wedding/celebrations or any other event @ Rs. 2,50,000 in addition to the room costs.
  • Area blocking charges for exclusive lunches or dinners is between Rs. 50,000/- and Rs.1,00000/-.
  • Conference equipment is provided as per actual costs, on prior notice.
  • No charge for child below 5 years and a half rates for child below 10 on meals. 11 years and above will be charged adult price.
  • Extra bed is available on requests. The cost of extra bed will be Rs. 2000/- plus taxes.
  • Breakfast is excluded / included in the room tariff , to be contacted with front desk team / Sales team depends upon your requirement & plan offered.
  • There are special charges for weekends, Christmas, and New year. Please do contact our Sales Offices for further information.
  • Rates for fashion photography and filming are available on request.
  • All prices mentioned regarding rooms, meals, services and other facilities are exclusive of taxes. CGST and SGST rates as per the GST Act and GST Rules & Regulations will be levied on the corresponding services availed at the properties. All taxes are subject to change as per the Government regulations from time to time.

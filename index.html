Enjoy a Luxury Experience

Hukam’s Lalit Mahal was established in 2018 and has created a name for itself as one of the best 5-star hotels in Raipur. We are a team of enthusiasts passionate about making each day memorable for all our guests. With the mantra of ‘living each day to its fullest’ embedded in our minds, it’s always a pleasure for us to know that we are a part of million memories for people across the World.

Hukam’s Lalit mahal is a pioneer in the Hospitality industry in Central India, especially in Chhattisgarh. It is the brain child of our founder, Mr. Lalit Kumar Jain, a veteran in the Hospitality industry, with more than 15 years of experience. Hukam’s Lalit Mahal is committed to bring you the best of unforgettable experiences, every day. Immerse yourself in the world of majestic opulence, as you enter the beauty of our magnificent premises, amongst the calmness of nature, with our courteous staff extending a warm welcome to you and your loved ones.
